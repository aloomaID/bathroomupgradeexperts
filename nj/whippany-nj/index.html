Whippany, NJ Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Whippany is a unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) located within Hanover Township in Morris County, in the U.S. state of New Jersey. Whippany's name is derived from the Whippanong Native Americans, a tribe that once inhabited the area. Whippanong meant 'place of the willows', named for the trees growing along the banks of the Whippany River.
