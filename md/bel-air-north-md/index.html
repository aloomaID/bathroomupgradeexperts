Bel Air North, MD Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Bel Air North is located in west-central Harford County at 39°33′2″N 76°21′42″W (39.550439, −76.361781). It is bordered to the south by the town of Bel Air and the Bel Air South CDP. It is bordered to the southwest by Fallston. Maryland Route 23 and Prospect Mill Road form the northern border of Bel Air North.
