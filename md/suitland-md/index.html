Suitland, MD Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Suitland is a suburb of Prince George's County, Maryland, approximately one mile (1.6 km) southeast of Washington, D.C. Rich in history and culture, Suitland offers plenty for residents and visitors to explore. Additionally, Suitland is a census designated place (CDP), as of the 2020 census, its population was 25,839.
