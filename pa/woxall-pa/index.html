Woxall, PA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Woxall or sometimes Woxhall is a census-designated place (CDP) in Upper Salford Township, Montgomery County, Pennsylvania, United States. It is located on the eastern side of the Perkiomen Creek, which flows south into the Schuylkill River. The population was 1,318 at the 2010 census. The CDP is served by the Harleysville post office, with the zip code of 19438.
